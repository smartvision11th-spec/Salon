Our Story

Founded in 2015, Lumière Salon has established itself as the premier destination for discerning clients seeking exceptional beauty services. Our team of internationally trained stylists and therapists brings together expertise from Paris, London, and New York.

We believe that true beauty emerges from the harmony of skill, premium products, and personalized attention. Every visit is curated to be an experience of relaxation, transformation, and renewal.
